What is Forex Account Management?
         
Forex account management is a service where a professional money manager trades a client's forex account on their behalf — executing strategy, managing risk, and generating returns while the client retains full ownership of the funds.
Managed forex accounts are used by passive investors, high-net-worth individuals, and experienced traders who want professional execution without the time commitment of active trading.
